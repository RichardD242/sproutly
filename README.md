# Sproutly

**Sproutly** is a simple and fun web app that lets you grow a virtual garden (IT grows even when you're **OFFLINE!**). Whether you love plants or just want to learn how gardening works, Sproutly helps you grow, care for, and enjoy your own little garden online.

---

## What is Sproutly?

Sproutly lets you:

- Plant seeds and watch them grow over time  
- Water and take care of your plants  
- Track sunlight, growth, and other garden stats  
- Collect different types of plants  
- Customize your garden layout

---

## How It Works
(You will start with 10 carrot seeds)

1. Choose a seed to plant  
2. Place it in your garden  
3. Give it water and sunlight  
4. Watch it grow
5. Sell it for **Sproukels** 
6. Add more plants to expand your garden

## What are **Sproukels**?

When you sell a fruit, you will be awarded with **Sproukels**, depending on size, mutation, rarity and etc. (About this later)
How can I use Sproukels?
1. You can buy more seeds at the Seed Shop
2. You can buy more equipment which helps you on your journey
3. You can get cosmetics and decoration for your garden
