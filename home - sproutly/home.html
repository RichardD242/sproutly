Planting and Removing your Plants

To place your seeds, simply select and select your desired spot.

To expand your garden, buy seeds that are currently in the seed shop.

Sell

To get Sproukels, harvest your fully grown plant in your garden.

Sproukels is the currency you will be awarded with, when selling your fruit. To buy seeds and equipment, you will also need this.
